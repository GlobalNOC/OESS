***WARNING!*** By enabling a loopback on this circuit, forwarding will be disrupted! All traffic in and out of this VLAN will be sent back to its originating point.


[% INCLUDE html_templates/map_legend.html %]

Select a node on either the primary path or the backup path. Once selected, traffic will go from the originating point, to the selected loopback node, and back to its originating point.


To remove the loopback from the circuit, return to the circuit details page, and click on Remove Circuit, which will remove the loop back point, and return you to this page.
